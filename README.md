# Life Insurance BRD Training

Participant materials for a short hands-on exercise on using ChatGPT to build a Business Requirements Document (BRD) from an annotated template.

The exercise uses a life-insurance scenario: **Stale Quote / Illustration Warning**. Participants follow along as ChatGPT interviews a stakeholder, clarifies requirements, generates a clean mini-BRD, and turns the process into reusable BRD Assistant instructions.

## What to use during the session

1. Open [`SETUP.md`](SETUP.md) before the session and confirm you can access ChatGPT.
2. Open the worksheet in [`Participant_Worksheet.docx`](Participant_Worksheet.docx).
3. Upload [`templates/BRD_Template_20_Min_Demo.md`](templates/BRD_Template_20_Min_Demo.md) into ChatGPT for the live exercise.
4. Keep [`prompts/chatgpt-prompts.md`](prompts/chatgpt-prompts.md) available for copy/paste prompts.
5. Use [`exercises/stale-quote-illustration-warning.md`](exercises/stale-quote-illustration-warning.md) as the scenario brief.

## What participants will produce

- A clean mini-BRD for the Stale Quote / Illustration Warning scenario
- A list of assumptions and open questions
- Draft reusable instructions for a BRD Assistant, Skill, or Custom GPT

## Notes

These are participant materials only. Instructor runbooks, timing cues, answer keys, and facilitator notes are intentionally excluded.
